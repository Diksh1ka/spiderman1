Spider-Man

About Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel,
created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 (August 1962)
Source: Marvel & Wikipedia
